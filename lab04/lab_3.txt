b)
Goal: P(c|+)
P(+|c) = 0.8
P(c) = 0.01
P(+|!c) = 0.096
P(!c) = 0.99 

P(+) = 0.99*0.096+0.01*0.8 = 0.103

P(c|+) = P(+|c)*P(c)/P(+) = 0.8*0.01/0.103 = 0.078
